About

AWE (Achieving Women Equity Fund) aims to transform communities, drive sustainable economic development, and deliver superior financial returns by leveraging HER power.

Achieving Women Equity (AWE) and the accompanying empowerment of women drives gender equitable growth which is sustainable, scalable and a positive impact for EVERYONE in the economy. The world will add $ 28 Trillion by investing in UN SDG #5.

AWE Impact Goal: Empowering 30 Million Women by 2030 by promoting:
Women Entrepreneurs
Women Corporate Leaders
Women Market Makers
Women Workforce
Women Consumers
